Chicken burrito recipe

Description

These easy 30 minute Chicken Burritos are stuffed with an incredibly delicious and perfectly seasoned chicken filling, Mexican rice, refried beans, corn and lots of cheese. If you have some leftovers, they’re perfect for freezing so you can enjoy some later.

This is a recipe that I’ve had on the blog for almost a decade now so I hope you enjoy it as much as we do!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Water or chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons avocado oil divided
  • ¼ cup green chiles
  • ¾ cup fire-roasted tomatoes
  • ¼ cup tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons taco seasoning store-bought or make your own
  • ½ cup corn
  • ½ cup refried beans store-bought or make your own
  • 4 flour tortillas 10-inch tortillas or larger
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese
  • guacamole optional, for serving

Instructions

  1. Place chicken breasts in pot and add enough water (or chicken broth) to fully cover chicken breasts.
  2. Bring liquid to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until chicken is no longer pink, approximately 10 minutes.
  3. Bring liquid to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until chicken is no longer pink, approximately 10 minutes.
  4. In large skillet, heat one tablespoon avocado oil over medium-high heat. When oil is hot, add shredded chicken.
  5. Reduce heat to medium. Stir in green chiles, fire-roasted tomatoes, and tomato sauce. Simmer 3 to 4 minutes.
  6. Reduce heat further to simmer. Stir in taco seasoning.
  7. Heat tortillas in microwave for approximately 15 seconds. See Notes for tips.
  8. Remove tortillas from microwave and lay out on flat surface. Layer each tortilla with refried beans, shredded chicken mixture, and shredded cheese. Tightly roll tortillas into burritos.
  9. Heat tortillas in microwave for approximately 15 seconds. See Notes for tips.
  10. Remove tortillas from microwave and lay out on flat surface. Layer each tortilla with refried beans, shredded chicken mixture, and shredded cheese. Tightly roll tortillas into burritos.
  11. Serve immediately with a side of guacamole (optional).
